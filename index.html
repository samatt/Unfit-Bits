About

Is your job preventing you from qualifying for health insurance discounts?
Do you have little time for exercise or limited access to sports facilities?
Are you are unable to afford active adventure holidays?
Or maybe you just want to keep your personal data private without having to pay higher insurance premiums for the privilege?

Fit Bit Spoofing provides a solution. This research project investigates several DIY Fitbit spoofing techniques enabling you to create walking datasets to qualify you for all the benefits of a high exercise lifestyle without actually having to share your personal data with your insurance company.
Our team of experts have conducted an in-depth FitBit Audit to understand how the Fitbit interprets data.
With these simple techniques using everyday devices from your home, we show you how to spoof your walking dataset so that you can qualify for the best discounts.
These techniques can also be used to obsficate your actual behaviors to help you to keep your privacy intact.
